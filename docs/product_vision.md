# Product Vision

**"To become the definitive Company Brain Infrastructure for modern product teams."**

Our vision extends naturally beyond the constraints of "AI-first chat." We are building infrastructure sitting squarely at the intersection of search, knowledge, and workflow automation. This category framing unlocks a significantly larger Total Addressable Market (TAM). 

When users adopt our platform, the onboarding must be an insane "Memory Magic" experience. Within their first 5 minutes, teams should feel: *"This system knows more about our company than we do, and it's already helping us focus."* We guarantee this by:
*   Automatically ingesting GitHub and Linear histories,
*   Automatically ranking the current workspace noise via the **Eisenhower Matrix**,
*   Automatically surfacing hidden architectural decisions and syncing context between teams.

Our free and open-source model allows even 1-person teams to immediately benefit from a proactive workspace agent. As organizations scale, our gamified freemium model unlocks advanced coordination and specialized proactive automations tailored for **50+ person product teams**.

Finally, we flip the open-core narrative completely. We exist to give technical leaders absolute control over their environment. We aren't just pitching "self-hosting for privacy." We offer **self-hosting for AI infrastructure cost control and customization**—giving enterprise buyers the ultimate freedom to train specialized foundation models entirely on their proprietary organization data.
